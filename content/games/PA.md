## Overview
Planet Amvlys was a student project I created for my game design course at the University of East London. I was the lead designer on the project within a team of 6 students. The project was developed within unreal engine 5 from September 2025 - May 2026.

My role for the project, was to write the story, concept all items, upgrades, and enemy's, as well as designing the level and all of its unique player interactions such as puzzles and lore logs. throughout this project, I learned quite a lot about team management, as well as further developing my understanding of UE5
