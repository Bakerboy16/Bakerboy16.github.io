## Overview
For a University project I was given the chance to write and direct a new mocap scene for a pre existing game as an imaginary DLC. this was all made possible via a collaboration between The university of east London and Target3D. The game I choose to write this imaginary DLC cutscene for was Metal Gear Solid 3. The scene I wrote, was about two of the games antagonists, Revolver Ocelot, and The Boss, getting a moment alone together and finally confronting each other about being mother and son, and why The Boss left her son.
