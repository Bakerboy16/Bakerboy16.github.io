Welcome to my portfolio!

I'm a final year game design student at the university of east London, I am highly passionate about level design, and Systems design. In my free time I like to work on small projects to help develop my skills further, as well as learning new skills, these projects can be viewed [Here](/Games). I have experience in working in professional fields with like minded people, all wanting to achieve the same goal. I work well within teams, and always try to help if others are stuck or have hit a creative block. I am a very self motivated developer, who loves to see the final outcome of the projects I work on.

I have a lot of experience in Unreal Engine blueprints, as well as a lot of experience in Unity and C#. in my free time I try to further my knowledge on both game engines, as well as learn new skills. Currently in my free time I am trying to get into music production as well as learning the Piano. My main focus Is level and systems design, with level taking a priority. I personally like to make sure the level fits the themes of the game, as well as telling a story through its details, and making sure its fun to play.

I am a highly passionate developer who is dedicated to making sure the experiences I work on are fun for the player, and leave them with thought provoking questions after the experience that stay with them.

# Experience
I have a high level of experience and understanding in both Unity and Unreal game engines. I have worked on many solo projects in Unity, and mainly group projects in unreal. In unity I've worked on a variety of different projects from [FPS proof of concepts](/Games/FPSD) to [horror game concepts](/Games/HGD). in unreal engine I've worked on two different projects, the first project I worked on was a group project that unfortunately never went into full production, it was a third person medieval fantasy rogue like about trying to get too the center of the earth. not everyone on the project was able too fully commit to development due to personal reasons, so we were only able to get a proof of concept made for it. The other project In unreal is still in development, The working title is "Planet Amvlys" Its a first person 3d Metroid Vania, where the remains of humanity are searching for a new planet to live.

I also have some experience within Godot. In February 2026 I took part in the UKIE game jam with both [Abby Smith](https://just-a-bee.github.io/), and [Dexter love](https://www.linkedin.com/in/dexter-love-285357257).The theme of the jam was symmetry, together we created a sokoban style game called Mirari where you play as a mirror and must reflect boxes to solve puzzles.
